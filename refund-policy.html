Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

We strive for complete customer satisfaction. This policy explains our refund process.

100% REFUND

Available if:

  • You cancel before we start work on your project
  • We're unable to start your project within 7 days of payment
  • There's a billing error on our part

Processing time: 5-10 business days via PayPal

PARTIAL REFUND

Available if:

  • Project is cancelled after work has started but before completion
  • Refund amount = Payment - (Hours worked × R200/hour)
  • We'll provide documentation of work completed

NO REFUND

Applies when:

  • Website is completed and delivered
  • You change your mind after approving design/features
  • For monthly management services after the service period has started
  • Domain names or third-party services already purchased

Management Plan Cancellations

  • Monthly Plans: Cancel anytime through PayPal
  • No Refund: For current month of service
  • Service Continues: Until end of paid period
  • No Auto-renewal: After cancellation

How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact Us: Email SASites@protonmail.com with "Refund Request" in subject
  2. Include: Your name, order date, and reason for refund
  3. Response Time: We'll respond within 48 business hours
  4. Approval: If approved, we'll process through PayPal
  5. Timeline: Refunds appear in 5-10 business days

Dispute Resolution

If you're unsatisfied with our refund decision:

  • We encourage open communication to find a solution
  • You may file a dispute through PayPal (within 180 days)
  • We'll provide all documentation to PayPal
  • PayPal's decision is final for payment disputes

Special Circumstances

  • Technical Issues: We'll fix any bugs or issues at no extra cost
  • Misunderstanding: Contact us first - we want to make it right
  • Dissatisfaction: We'll work with you to meet expectations

Contact for Refund Questions

  • Email: SASites@protonmail.com
  • Phone: +27 73 088 2767
  • Response Time: 24-48 business hours

Note: This policy complies with South African Consumer Protection Act.
